行列式具有分行可加性，即
$$
\begin{align}
&\begin{vmatrix}
a_{11}&a_{12}&\cdots&a_{1n}\\
\vdots&\vdots&&\vdots\\
b_{l1}+c_{l1}&b_{l2}+c_{l2}&\cdots&b_{ln}+c_{ln}\\
\vdots&\vdots&&\vdots\\
a_{n1}&a_{n2}&\cdots&a_{nn}
\end{vmatrix}\\
=
&\begin{vmatrix}
a_{11}&a_{12}&\cdots&a_{1n}\\
\vdots&\vdots&&\vdots\\
b_{l1}&b_{l2}&\cdots&b_{ln}\\
\vdots&\vdots&&\vdots\\
a_{n1}&a_{n2}&\cdots&a_{nn}
\end{vmatrix}+
\begin{vmatrix}
a_{11}&a_{12}&\cdots&a_{1n}\\
\vdots&\vdots&&\vdots\\
c_{l1}&c_{l2}&\cdots&c_{ln}\\
\vdots&\vdots&&\vdots\\
a_{n1}&a_{n2}&\cdots&a_{nn}
\end{vmatrix}
\end{align}
$$
